MY STORY

2019. The year I started reading novels.

My first novel that introduced me to this world was about the stock market. I remember it very precisely; it was "Trading For A Living". I was in 12th grade at that time and I was really confused about what I wanted to do and I was exploring my options. It's kind of unclear to me how and when I got into fiction novels. I try to remember it a lot of times but I can't recall it at all. I even made notes of my first novel and I still have them for future advice. ;)

I read a lot and realized that what I choose was I wanted to do. I didn't knew what web development was at that time but I had ideas. This site was also one of the dreams that I painted at that time and I'm happy I made it at last.

Everyone was making relationship a big deal. I knew they just wanted to tick off a mark in their life. Most of them were not even serious thank god i doughed those bullets. Never thought I would be this relieved that I dated in school.

2022. 2nd year.

I only wanted to focus on myself and started making this project. It looked hilarious back then and I'm glad I improved it.
